SAMBA: The Sentient Ambient Monitoring of Buildings in Australia

A World-First, Real-Time, and Low-Cost device that measures the Indoor Environmental Quality (IEQ). SAMBA can dynamically measure, monitor, and report IEQ parameters: allowing you to respond efficiently to any room environment issues. Perfect for the fast-paced, patient-first environments of healthcare facilities.

What do we measure?

Temperature

Helping staff members to regulate the patient's room temperature- leading to happier patients, and a better work environment for staff!

Humidity

Helping staff members to know the humidity of the patient's room so they can better manage the patients health, safety, and wellbeing.

Light

Which helps staff to know about any light disturbances, particularly at night, so they help to manage the sleeping schedule of their patient.

Sound

Which alerts staff to any sound issues, helping them to reduce the amount of sound to manage the sleep and wellbeing of their patients.

CO2

Helping staff members to maintain adequate ventelation of the patient's room, supporting their wellbeing and healing process!
